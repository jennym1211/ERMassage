Welcome!

Welcome to Escape & Revitalize Massage! Have a kink in your shoulders that refuses to go away? Or maybe don't get a chance to relax often? Life gets busy, tough and unpredictable. You deserve all the time and self care you can dive on in to.

Hi! My name is Brianna Jansen, I am the owner and massage therapist for Escape & Revitalize Massage.

Wanting to pursue my passion for helping people, I originally attended Tarleton State University to become a nurse, but I decided to change career paths along the way. With the same goal in mind, in 2018, I got my massage license and have been practicing since.

I whole-heartedly believe that self-care is a huge MUST in the daily hustle and bustle of everyday life. I know I'm new to the massage scene, but let my hands do the talking!

I specialize in deep tissue techniques, myofascial release and therapeutic techniques to bring you a completely customized session. And for those that want to relax, don't worry I can do swedish for you too. Hot towels are included in every massage for no extra charge. One massage does not fit all and with all the choices out there, I appreciate you choosing Escape & Revitalize Massage.
